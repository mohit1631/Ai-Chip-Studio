The Workflow

Every stage a real chip design goes through

Not a toy simulator — the same Verilator, Yosys, and OpenROAD a tapeout team would reach for, wired together and watched over by AI at each handoff.

Stage 01 — RTL & Verification

Write Verilog, catch bugs before they're bugs

A full editor with live linting, AI-assisted review, and waveform inspection side by side — so the gap between "I wrote this" and "I know this works" stays small.

  • Static + AI-powered RTL review
  • Verilator & Icarus simulation
  • Integrated waveform viewer
silicon-lint.studio
Stage 02 — Synthesis

RTL becomes gates, instantly inspectable

Yosys transforms your design into a gate-level netlist; we render the resulting schematic and timing report so the translation from intent to hardware isn't a black box.

  • Yosys-based logic synthesis
  • Schematic & timing visualization
  • Area / power estimates pre-layout
synthesis-studio
Stage 03 — Place & Route

Real layout, real PPA, real PDK

OpenROAD floorplans, places, and routes your netlist against an actual process design kit — so the power, performance, and area numbers you get back are the ones that matter.

  • SKY130 & GF180MCU PDK support
  • Floorplan → CTS → routing → signoff
  • DRC-checked, downloadable GDSII
openroad · sky130hd
